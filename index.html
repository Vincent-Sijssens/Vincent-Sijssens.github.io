Reverb

Reverb is an open-source, event-driven, asynchronous workflow engine that orchestrates the logic and infrastructure developers need to build complex background tasks.

Users define tasks as multi-step functions in their repository, and Reverb handles triggering, executing, and retrying them step-by-step. The service is self-hosted on AWS and is deployable with one command.
